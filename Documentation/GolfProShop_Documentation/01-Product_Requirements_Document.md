# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Vision
GolfProShop is an AI-powered Apple Watch and iPhone golf companion that improves golfer performance through GPS, weather, shot analytics and personalized coaching.

## Objectives
- Deliver a frictionless on-course experience.
- Learn from every shot.
- Provide explainable club and aim recommendations.
- Operate offline during a round.

## Primary Users
- Recreational golfers
- Club members
- Coaches
- Golf clubs

## Functional Requirements
- Player profile
- Club management
- Course management
- Round management
- Shot recording
- GPS tracking
- Weather integration
- AI recommendations
- Post-round analytics
- Sharing

## Non-functional Requirements
- Native Apple experience
- Offline-first
- Privacy-first
- Battery efficient
- Secure CloudKit sync
